   INTLIST(x) -> IF_INTLIST(empty(x), x)
The remaining pairs can at least be oriented weakly.
Used ordering:  Polynomial interpretation [POLO,RATPOLO]:

   POL(IF_INTLIST(x_1, x_2)) = [1/4]x_1 + x_2
   POL(INTLIST(x_1)) = [1/4] + [2]x_1
   POL(cons(x_1, x_2)) = [1] + [4]x_2
   POL(empty(x_1)) = [1/4] + [2]x_1
   POL(false) = [1]
   POL(nil) = 0
   POL(tail(x_1)) = [1/2]x_1
